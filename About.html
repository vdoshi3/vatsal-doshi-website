BUDT748 - INDUSTRY PRACTICUM

BUDT748 serves as the culminating experience for Master of Science Information Systems students, providing hands-on opportunities to create innovative technological solutions for industry partners. This capstone program emphasizes end-to-end project development, from initial concept through final deployment, while fostering critical thinking and technical expertise essential for tomorrow's technology leaders.
